Our music center offers six rehearsal rooms - tailor-made for a community of creative and professional musicians, bands, and solo practitioners. Everyone is welcome!

You play guitar but aren't in a band? No problem. You love classical music but can't host a grand piano at home? You're in the right place. You want to practice saxophone but don't want to annoy anyone at home? Come on in and meet others just like you!

Sooner or later, you'll meet fellow artists here. Just register and book your room!

Our Rooms

LARGE ROOMS

big bands

We have two big rooms. Up to 10 people have a place here, whether to perform or just enjoy. One drum set, 4 guitar amps, 2 bass amps, 2 synths, several mics and small sofa. Interested?
More...

MEDIUM ROOMS

the classic

4 rooms, suitable for up to 5 people. Ideal for rehearsal, jamming, or writing sessions. Comfortable chairs for creative exchange, A drum set, guitar/ bass amps and microphones are standard — two rooms also include synthesizers. One room stands out with a grand piano (Flügel).
More...

SMALL ROOMS

take your time

The brain cells of our center. Our two smaller rooms are perfect for focused practice, with space for up to 3 people. One features an upright piano, the other a drum set. Both are equipped with two chairs to encourage creative or pedagogical exchange.
